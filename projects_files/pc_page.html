''CRITICAL MASS'' is available on special limited edition Compact Disc, 12' Vinyl Record and a Digital Download. The sound is visualised by kinetic sculpture, shot as a sequence from a futuristic film, the sterile, pulsing and dynamic tone of the music is being translated into exciting surreal images.
